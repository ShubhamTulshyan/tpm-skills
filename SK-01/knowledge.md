# SK-01: Mechanism Selection

## Purpose
Selects the optimal promotional mechanism (price discount, bundle offer, gift-with-purchase, loyalty incentive, etc.) based on business objectives, market conditions, and target audience characteristics.

## Key Responsibilities
- Evaluate available promotional mechanisms
- Match mechanisms to business goals
- Consider market context and customer segments
- Recommend mechanism with highest strategic fit

## Data Inputs
- Business objectives
- Market conditions
- Customer segment profiles
- Competitive landscape

## Output
- Recommended mechanism
- Rationale for selection
- Expected mechanism effectiveness
